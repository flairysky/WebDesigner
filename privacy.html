Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Glowy Design Studio ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you visit our website or engage our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name and email address submitted through our contact form.
  • Project details — information you voluntarily share about yourself and your project.
  • Usage data — anonymised analytics data (pages visited, time on site, referral source) collected to help us improve our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and deliver our services.
  • Understand how our website is used and continuously improve it.
  • Communicate project updates and relevant information with active clients.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

3. Analytics

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with our website. Microsoft Clarity is a behavioural analytics tool that may record mouse movements, clicks, scrolls, and page navigation to help us improve the content and usability of our site. This data is collected only with your consent, which you can grant or withdraw at any time via the cookie banner on our site.

Microsoft may also use the data collected by Clarity for their own purposes in accordance with Microsoft's Privacy Statement. By accepting analytics cookies on our site, you agree that both we and Microsoft may collect and use this data.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to gather analytics data through Microsoft Clarity. When you first visit our site, you will be asked for your consent before any tracking cookies are set. You may:

  • Accept — analytics cookies are enabled and Microsoft Clarity will begin tracking your session.
  • Reject — no analytics cookies are set and Microsoft Clarity will not be loaded.

You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site. You may also disable cookies entirely in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Microsoft Clarity — behavioural analytics. Data is processed by Microsoft in accordance with the Microsoft Privacy Statement. Clarity is only activated with your explicit consent.
  • Formspree — contact form processing. Formspree may store and process submitted contact data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at formspree.io.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or fulfil our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly.

8. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our contact form.
